BIOGRAPHY

Idris Ibrahim, popularly known as Pentest Warrior, is a cybersecurity practitioner, ethical hacking enthusiast, and cybersecurity educator dedicated to promoting digital security awareness and practical cybersecurity skills.

I specialize in ethical hacking fundamentals, OSINT investigation, phishing awareness, web security, and penetration testing practices. With strong passion for cybersecurity education, Idris actively teaches beginners and non-technical individuals about online safety, cyber threats, and responsible ethical hacking practices.

As a Certified Cybersecurity Educator Professional (CCEP), he combines technical knowledge with practical teaching experience to simplify complex cybersecurity concepts for students, aspiring ethical hackers, and organizations.

My mission is to empower individuals and businesses with cybersecurity awareness, practical defensive skills, and responsible security practices in today's digital world.
